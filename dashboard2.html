Courses

Course content

Includes a counter for number of courses, a button to create a new course, then in a list of categories: All, Drafts, Published, Archived, Templates, and Archived Templates. Included on a screen of empty courses is a specific button to create your first course with some text. The text explains what this area of the dashboard this is, and how you can use other features like drafts and publishing courses, archived courses, and templates.

Upon pressing a button to create a new course, a group of steps to make a complete course appears, with the first step shown, comprising of a form. The first form is the course's details including the Course's name, the primary language, the author, a description, an activation (introductory) message up to 500 characters, and a cover image max 1280x1139 px. The service can be stopped by texting STOP, and help can be received by responding with SUPPORT.

The next step comprises of creating the individual lessons for this course. A list of empty lessons is loaded that can be filled by the user. Each lesson is made of an image preview 1280x1139 px, its Name, and a body of text. Follow up questions can be added, either free response or multiple choice. From there on the user can save the changes to the lesson, or continue on to delivery and distribution configuration options.

Delivery

delivery to users

Distribution settings for how to organize sending content to users subscribed to a course. Included in the form for a course is the distribution name, the course name to distribute, the selected delivery methods (sms, whatsapp, email), preferred delivery method, preferred country, course cadence (scheduled, triggered by X, etc), scheduling (which days, times, whether to deliver anytime or deliver at a specific time in your timezone, and if to keep sending whether or not responses from user are detected).

These disttributions are shown as a list with categories for its name, date created, cohorts, enrollment options, edit, and delete. Learners can enroll using a link, or QR code

Learners

Users, students, etc

Go to a list of all your students, grouped by the course theyre subscribed to. We should be able to see different courses' students, and be able to edit, delete, reset or change their progress, and other general management.

Reporting

Show analytics, completion, confidence, etc

Record every time a user completes or answers certain questions and finishes certain courses, and compare that data to other users also enrolled in that course. Track the amount of courses a user might be subscribed to, the number of correct answers, incorrect answers, saved answers, or answers skipped total.
